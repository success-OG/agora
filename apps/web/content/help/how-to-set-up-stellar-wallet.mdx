# How to set up a Stellar Wallet (Freighter, Albedo)

Connecting to Agora requires a compatible Stellar wallet. A wallet is a piece of software that securely holds your funds (like XLM and USDC) and allows you to interact with decentralized applications without sharing your private keys.

## Recommended Wallets

We recommend using one of the following wallets:
- **[Freighter](https://freighter.app/)**: A browser extension wallet built by the Stellar Development Foundation.
- **[Albedo](https://albedo.link/)**: A web-based wallet that doesn't require any installation.

## Step-by-Step Guide: Setting up Freighter

1. **Install the Extension:** Visit the [Freighter website](https://freighter.app/) and install the extension for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Brave).
2. **Create a New Wallet:** Open the Freighter extension and click on **Create Wallet**.
3. **Save Your Recovery Phrase:** You will be shown a 12-word or 24-word recovery phrase. **Write this down on a piece of paper and store it securely.** Never share this phrase with anyone. If you lose it, you lose access to your funds.
4. **Set a Password:** Create a strong password. This password will be used to unlock the extension on your current device.
5. **Fund Your Wallet:** To interact with the Stellar network, your wallet needs a minimum balance of XLM (Stellar Lumens) to cover network reserves. You can transfer XLM from an exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken to your Freighter public address.

## Connecting to Agora

1. Navigate to the Agora platform.
2. Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner.
3. Select **Freighter** (or Albedo) from the list of options.
4. Your wallet will prompt you to approve the connection. Once approved, you are ready to buy tickets!
