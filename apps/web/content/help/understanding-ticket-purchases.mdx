# Understanding Ticket Purchases with Stellar USDC

On Agora, tickets are purchased using **USDC on the Stellar network**. USDC is a stablecoin pegged 1-to-1 with the US Dollar, ensuring that ticket prices remain stable and predictable.

## Why Stellar USDC?

- **Stability:** The price of 1 USDC is always $1 USD.
- **Speed:** Transactions on the Stellar network confirm in roughly 3 to 5 seconds.
- **Low Cost:** Network fees are a tiny fraction of a cent.

## Step-by-Step Guide: Buying a Ticket

1. **Ensure You Have Funds:** Make sure your connected wallet (e.g., Freighter) holds enough **Stellar USDC** to cover the ticket price, plus a small amount of **XLM** to cover the transaction fee.
2. **Navigate to the Event:** Go to the event page you wish to attend.
3. **Select Your Ticket:** Choose the ticket tier and quantity.
4. **Click "Buy Ticket":** This will initiate a transaction request to your wallet.
5. **Approve the Transaction:** Your wallet extension will pop up. Review the total cost (USDC + XLM fee) and click **Approve** or **Sign**.
6. **Confirmation:** Within a few seconds, the transaction will be processed on the blockchain. You will receive your digital ticket directly in your wallet!

> Note: If you don't have Stellar USDC, you can acquire it on major cryptocurrency exchanges and withdraw it directly to your Stellar wallet over the Stellar network.
